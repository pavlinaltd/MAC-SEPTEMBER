Macedonian Folk Songs Reinterpreted

Partings (Album)

Produced by the Macedonian Arts Council (MAC) and published by Kukuzel Inc

...Leaving their wives and youngsters behind and seeking quick riches in the new land, they took off by the hundreds, knowing that what they would earn in America in a few years, would last them a lifetime back home. Most of them would eventually return to their families, with their life savings, but robbed of their youth and now strangers to their already grown children... For thousands of men of Macedonia, this life was reality, and Partings tells their stories through the musical interpretations of some of the world’s best contemporary musicians.
Each of the 11 songs originated in Macedonian folklore, but have now been reimagined by composers and performers hailing from countries like India, Wales, Germany and Ecuador. Many of these artists chose to include instruments from their native land, melding Macedonian musical culture into their own.
All proceeds from Partings will benefit the Stoyan Christowe Endowment Fund, which supports young Macedonian women who do not have the means to pursue a college education.

Lilts (Coming Soon!!)

Produced by the Macedonian Arts Council (MAC)

The MACEDONIAN ARTS COUNCIL is proud to present “LILTS” - the second in a tryptic of musical albums that present Macedonian musical culture through folk songs, in modern arrangements that can be shared with world music lovers. ...Stoyan's father wove a basket Made of pine straws, brightly colored And placed it by the fire So his mother can lull her first born Baby Stoyan, their little new born. Sleep my darling son In the warmth of your pretty crib Your mother will sing to you, Your father, will lull you We’ll both watch over you….. We’ll both watch you grow..... "Father Wove a Crib" is a macedonian lullaby arranged and performed by the amazing singer-songwriter Sophie Bernice.
The original melodies for LILTS were recorded acapella, in villages throughout Macedonia, and have been the basis for the new arrangements. Our collaborators come from Italy, Sardinia, the UK, France, Sweden, the US and Japan. we are previewing these songs from the upcoming album, slated for release at end of the fall 2023.

In Distress

Currently In-production by the Macedonian Arts Council (MAC)

Prepare to embark on a musical journey like no other as we bring you 'IN DISTRESS,' a forthcoming album that pays homage to the rich tapestry of 19th-century Macedonian women's folk songs. ...the woman has suffered more than most other human beings ...she had been born during the calamitous end of Turkish maladministration... then in her maturity had come the Balkan wars and the Great War with cholera and typhus epidemic in between...she has had far less of anything, of personal possessions, of security, of care of childbirth than any western woman can imagine... but she had two possessions which any western woman might envy... she had strength, the terrible stony strength of Macedonia...
'IN DISTRESS' is currently in the throes of production, where melodies are being meticulously crafted, and stories from the past are being given a modern twist. If you're eager to be part of this enchanting musical odyssey, we invite you to get in touch with us below. Your support and enthusiasm will help bring this extraordinary project to life.
